Privacy Policy

Welcome to our Library Management System! We are committed to protecting your personal information and your right to privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our system.

Information We Collect

When you register or use our Library Management System, we collect the following personal information:

  • Username
  • Email address
  • OTP for email verification
  • Password (hashed and securely stored)
  • First Name and Last Name
  • Phone Number
  • Profile Picture (optional)
  • Borrowing and request history

How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Register and manage user accounts
  • Process book borrowing requests
  • Send notifications and alerts (e.g., due date reminders, OTP for verification)
  • Ensure the security of user accounts
  • Improve our services and user experience

How We Protect Your Information

We implement security measures to protect your personal data, including:

  • Secure password storage using hashing (no plain text storage)

Sharing of Information

We do not share your personal information with third parties, except:

  • When required by law or legal processes
  • To protect the rights and safety of our users and the system

Your Privacy Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request corrections or updates to your data
